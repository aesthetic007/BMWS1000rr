BMW S1000rr



       BMW

(Bayerische Motoren Werke Aktiengesellschaft)


S1000RR is a race oriented sport bike initially made by BMW Motorrad to compete in the 2009 Superbike
World Championship,
that is now in commercial production. It was introduced in Munich in April 2008, and is powered by a 999 cc (61.0 cu in) four-cylinder engine redlined at 14,200 rpm.
